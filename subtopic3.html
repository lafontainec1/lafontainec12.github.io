Soccer VAR & Goal-Line Technology

Source 1

URLESPN: Semi‑Automated VAR Offside
AuthorDale Johnson
Abstract ESPN explains semi-automated offside technology (SAOT) in soccer, which uses AI and cameras to track player positions in real time. The system speeds up VAR decisions, reduces human error, and increases confidence in officiating. It allows referees to make more accurate calls while providing clear visualizations for fans, combining technology with traditional referee oversight for fairness.

Source 2

URLInside FIFA: Semi‑Automated Offside Tech
AuthorFIFA Innovation Team
Abstract FIFA details how tracking cameras and sensors in the ball detect offside in real time. The system monitors multiple points per player, predicts the kick point, and sends instant alerts to video match officials. It generates 3D visualizations for broadcasts, ensuring transparency and accuracy. This technology modernizes soccer, improving fairness without altering the essence of the game.
